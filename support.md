# CueID Support

For support, contact:

admin@kirigoe.uk

Please include:

- The version of CueID you are using
- Your macOS version
- The type of audio file you were scanning
- A short description of the issue
- Any error message shown in the app

## Common Notes

CueID results depend on the quality of the audio and whether the track is available in the recognition catalog.

Some live edits, remixes, transitions, short clips, layered audio, and noisy recordings may not be recognized.

Apple Music playlist creation requires Apple Music permission and access to Apple Music in your region.
